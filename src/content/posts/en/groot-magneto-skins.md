## Groot & Magneto New Skins Are Here!

Marvel Rivals has released the "Mecha-Flora" skin for Groot and the "Seat of Autumn" skin for Magneto.

These represent the final major costume additions for Season 6, ahead of the Season 6.5 launch scheduled for February 13, 2026.

---

## Release Date

**February 6, 2026 at 02:00 UTC**

Available for purchase in the in-game shop.

---

## Groot - Mecha-Flora

![Groot & Magneto Skins](/images/groot-magneto-skins.png)

Nature meets machine with this mechanical Groot transformation!

### Skin Features
- **Legendary Skin**
- **Custom Sound Effects**: Unique ability sounds
- **Robotic Voice Filter**: Mechanized voice modification
- **Green & Black Color Scheme**: Cyberpunk-inspired design

## Magneto - Seat of Autumn

The Master of Magnetism takes on an elegant autumn-inspired style!

- **Autumn Leaf Motifs**: Seasonal aesthetic
- **Throne-like Cape**: Commanding silhouette
