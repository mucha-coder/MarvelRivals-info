## New "Ace of Spades" & "Queen of Diamonds" Skins Are Here!

Marvel Rivals has dropped two stunning casino-themed skins for Angela and Emma Frost. These card game-inspired designs bring elegance and style to the battlefield.

## Release Date

**Available from January 30, 2026 at 2:00 UTC**

---

## Angela - Ace of Spades

![Angela & Emma Frost Skins](/images/angela-emma-skins.png)

The angelic warrior Angela transforms into a dark and elegant Ace of Spades!

### Skin Features
- **Dark Armor**: Black and gold color scheme
- **Spade Motifs**: Spade symbols on weapons and outfit
- **Casino-style Accessories**: Card-themed decorations

---

## Emma Frost - Queen of Diamonds

The White Queen Emma Frost reigns as the Queen of Diamonds!

### Skin Features
- **White & Gold Outfit**: Pure white and gold royal design
- **Diamond Tiara**: Sparkling crystal crown
- **Elegant Cape**: Graceful flowing cloak
