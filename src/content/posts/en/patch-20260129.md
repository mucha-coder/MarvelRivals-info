## Marvel Rivals Patch Notes - Build Version: 20260129

The latest update for Marvel Rivals was released on January 29, 2026, at 09:00 (UTC). This update brings a new map, a new mode, and numerous events without any server downtime.

### New Map: Museum of Contemplation
A new high-speed Escort map that takes players from the Collector's Museum to the Contemplator's Garden and finally to the Coliseum of the Elders. Deadpool takes the wheel as a school bus driver, transporting liberated creatures.

### New Mode: The Clobberin' Club
Deep beneath Times Square lies a secret fight club. This new mode offers 1v1 duels, providing the perfect stage to prove your individual skill.

### New Event: Flight Mode! World Tour
**Duration: Jan 29, 2026 - Feb 19, 2026**

Complete missions to earn Hela's "Disco of the Dead" costume for free!
Premium unlocks include Spider-Man's "Groovy Swing" and Psylocke's "Psychedelic Pulse" costumes.

### Times Square: Disco Night!
Lead dancers Psylocke, Hela, and Spider-Man have taken over the underground Disco in Times Square with their new outfits! Join the Disco and dance with them for the first time after the update to receive **100 Units**.
