The latest update for Marvel Rivals was released on March 5, 2026 at 09:00 UTC. No server downtime was required.

## 🔄 Chrono-Rush Event Returns!

**Duration: March 5, 2026 09:00 UTC – March 20, 2026 09:00 UTC** (2 weeks)

The popular Chrono-Rush event is back!

### Event Features
- Play matches to earn **Chrono Tokens** (no earning cap!)
- Use tokens to unlock Battle Pass tiers and cosmetics
- Surplus tokens can be exchanged for **Unity** (up to 1,500 Units)

Play as much as you can to stock up on tokens!

---

## 🔧 Fixes & Optimizations

### All Platforms

- **Iron Fist (Lion's Gaze Costume)**: Fixed an issue with the Savage Spirit and Lively Lion Chromas where an exclusive ultimate voice line persisted even when toggled off in settings. The voice toggle now works as intended.

### Hero-Specific Fixes

- **Angela**: Fixed a bug where Assassin's Charge sometimes wouldn't carry enemies if she collided with another Angela also using Assassin's Charge.
- **Doctor Strange**: Fixed an issue where enemies wouldn't immediately fall when a portal was conjured beneath their feet. Targets now fall as intended.
