## New "Disco Pop" & "Boogie Bolt" Skins Available for Limited Time!

Marvel Rivals has dropped two dazzling new skins for Luna Snow and Thor. These disco-themed cosmetics are available for a limited time in the in-game shop.

## Availability Period

**January 23, 2026 2:00 UTC - February 20, 2026 2:00 UTC**

> ⚠️ These are limited-time skins! Once the period ends, they will no longer be available for purchase. Get them while you can!

---

## Luna Snow - Disco Pop

![Luna Snow Disco Pop](/images/luna-snow-thor-skins.jpg)

K-pop idol Luna Snow transforms into a glittering disco queen!

### Skin Features
- **Mirror Ball Outfit**: Silver and pink glitter design
- **Neon Blue Hair**: Cyberpunk-style hair color
- **Star Accessories**: Sparkling star decorations on face

## Thor - Boogie Bolt

The God of Thunder brings funky disco vibes to the battlefield!

### Skin Features
- **Sunglasses**: Purple gradient shades
- **Boombox Mjolnir**: His hammer transforms into a retro radio!
- **70s Color Scheme**: Gold and electric blue styling
